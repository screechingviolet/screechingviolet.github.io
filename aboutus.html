About HOPE


HOPE has a vision of reducing food waste in Singapore while helping out those who struggle with access to food.

The Problem

Food waste has become a huge global issue in recent years. In the past decade, places such as Singapore have seen their food waste increase by over 40%. It’s having a tremendous toll on the environment and economy. This is why it’s so important that action is taken in order to reduce the amount of food waste
